<p><p>The Automotive Brake Actuation Systems market comprises three key types: Brake Booster, Tandem Master Cylinders (TMCs), and Brake Fluid Reservoir. Brake boosters enhance the braking force by reducing the pedal effort required, improving vehicle safety. Tandem Master Cylinders supply hydraulic pressure to each brake circuit individually, ensuring independent braking action. Brake fluid reservoirs store the brake fluid required for the hydraulic actuation system, preventing air or moisture from entering the system and maintaining its efficiency. These components play crucial roles in ensuring efficient and safe braking in automotive vehicles.</p></p>
