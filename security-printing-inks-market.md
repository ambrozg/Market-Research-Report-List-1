<p><p>Security printing inks refer to special inks used in the production of secure documents and packaging to prevent counterfeiting and unauthorized reproduction. Intaglio inks are used in intaglio printing, creating raised ink surfaces. Silkscreen inks are used in silkscreen printing, where ink is forced through a stencil onto the desired substrate. Letterpress inks are used in letterpress printing, where ink is transferred from raised surfaces. Offset inks are used in offset printing, where ink is transferred from a plate to a rubber blanket before being applied to the substrate. Other types of security printing inks include fluorescent inks and invisible inks.</p></p>
