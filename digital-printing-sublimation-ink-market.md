<p><p>Digital printing sublimation ink market can be categorized into two types: direct sublimation and transfer sublimation. </p><p>Direct sublimation involves printing the image directly onto the fabric using a printer that is specifically designed for sublimation. The ink is then heat-pressed into the fabric, resulting in vibrant and long-lasting prints.</p><p>Transfer sublimation, on the other hand, requires printing the image onto a transfer paper first. The printed image is then heat-transferred onto the fabric using a heat press machine, allowing the ink to penetrate the fabric and creating high-quality prints.</p></p>
