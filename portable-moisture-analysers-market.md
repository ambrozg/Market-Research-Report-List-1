<p><p>Portable Moisture Analysers are used to measure the moisture content present in various substances. The market for these devices can be categorized into different types. Microwave moisture analysers use microwave radiation to generate heat and determine moisture levels. Infrared moisture analysers use infrared light to measure moisture by analyzing the absorption and reflection properties of the substance. Loss on Drying (LOD) moisture analysers measure the moisture content by drying the substance and calculating the weight loss. Other types of moisture analysers use different techniques such as capacitance, resistive, or thermal conductivity to determine moisture levels.</p></p>
