<p><p>The closed-cell elastomeric foam market consists of various types, including NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based, EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based, chloroprene based, and others. NBR-based foam offers excellent oil and fuel resistance, making it suitable for applications in the automotive and industrial sectors. EPDM-based foam provides exceptional resistance to weather, UV, and ozone, making it ideal for outdoor applications like HVAC systems. Chloroprene-based foam exhibits good thermal insulation and flame retardant properties, making it widely used in construction and electrical industries. The "others" category includes various elastomeric foam types catering to specific requirements and niche applications.</p></p>
