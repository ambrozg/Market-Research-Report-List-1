<p><p>The 3D Printing Plastic and Photopolymer Material market comprises various types of printing technologies. Fused Deposition Modelling (FDM) involves melting and extruding plastic material onto a platform to create objects layer by layer. Stereolithography (SLA) uses a laser to solidify liquid resin, also layer by layer. Polyjet Printing uses multiple jetting heads to deposit resin materials and create objects with high precision. Laser Sintering employs lasers to fuse powdered materials, such as plastic or nylon, to shape the final product. Digital Light Processing (DLP) uses a projector and light-sensitive resin to create objects layer by layer. Other printing technologies also exist in this market.</p></p>
