<p><p>The aerospace wiring harness market encompasses various types catering to different sections of an aircraft. The wing wiring harness is responsible for connecting electrical systems within the wings, while the fuselage wiring harness is found throughout the main body of the aircraft. The empennage wiring harness supports the tail section. Interior wiring harnesses handle electrical needs within the cabin, while front section wiring harnesses are used in the aircraft's nose. Lastly, the engine wiring harness is responsible for electrical connections with the engine.</p></p>
