<p><p>Metal coating service market types include liquid spray, powder coating, and others. </p><p>Liquid spray involves the application of a liquid coating onto the metal surface. It is widely used for its flexibility, enabling the coating of complex shapes and sizes.</p><p>Powder coating, on the other hand, involves the application of a dry powder onto the metal surface. It then goes through a curing process, resulting in a durable and attractive finish. This method is known for its environmental friendliness and resistance to chipping.</p><p>The category of others encompasses various coating methods such as electroplating, electroless plating, and physical vapor deposition (PVD).
