There are two main types of bonded ferrite magnets on the market: injected magnets and pressed magnets. Injected magnets are made by injecting the magnet mixture into a mold, allowing for complex shapes and designs. Pressed magnets, on the other hand, are made by compressing the magnet mixture into a pre-formed shape. Both types offer advantages such as high magnetic performance, low cost, and resistance to corrosion and demagnetization, making them widely used in various industries.</p></p>
