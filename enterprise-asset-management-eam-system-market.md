<p><p>The Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) system market includes two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises solutions. Cloud-Based EAM systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage their assets via the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs. On the other hand, On-Premises EAM systems are installed and maintained within the organization's own infrastructure.
