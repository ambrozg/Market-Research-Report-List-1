<p><p>The carbomer gel market comprises two main types: chlorine-free copolymer and chlorine copolymer. These carbomers are considered to be more environmentally friendly and are often preferred for use in personal care and healthcare products. On the other hand, chlorine copolymer carbomers are produced using chlorine, and are commonly used in industrial applications due to their increased stability and resistance to degradation in harsher environments.
