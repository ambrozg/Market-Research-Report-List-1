<p><p>The Xylenes market consists of four types: Ortho Xylene, Meta Xylene, Para Xylene, and Mixed Xylene. Ortho Xylene is mainly used in the manufacturing of phthalic anhydride, which is essential for producing plasticizers and polyester resins. Meta Xylene is utilized in the production of solvents, paints, and coatings. Para Xylene is a key component in the manufacturing of purified terephthalic acid, which is essential for polyester production. Lastly, Mixed Xylene is used in the production of solvents, fuels, and other chemical intermediates. These different types cater to various industries, serving diverse purposes.</p></p>
