<p><p>The Automatic PET Blow Molder market includes various types of machines used for manufacturing PET bottles. Extrusion Blow Molders shape the bottles by extruding melted plastic through a die and then blowing air to expand it. Injection Blow Molders inject molten plastic into a mold cavity, which is then stretched and blown into the final shape. Stretch Blow Molders use preforms that are heated, stretched, and then blown into bottles. These machines cater to different production needs and are widely used in industries such as food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
