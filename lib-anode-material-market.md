<p><p>The LIB (Lithium-ion Battery) anode material market can be categorized into various types based on the composition and properties of the materials used. Synthetic graphite, on the other hand, is manufactured through the graphitization of various carbon-based materials.</p><p>Amorphous carbon includes materials that lack a crystalline structure, offering unique properties such as higher capacity and faster charge-discharge rates. Lithium Titanate (LTO) is another type of anode material that exhibits excellent cycling stability and safety characteristics.</p><p>Lastly, the "other" category encompasses other emerging anode materials such as silicon/graphite composites, lithium metal, and other carbon-based materials that are being researched and developed for improved battery performance and energy storage applications.</p></p>
